I remember the very first class when my teacher played a slideshow for us of various photographs he had taken from the internet.Most of them were you're typical "quality" photograph. A couple on a beach, a close up of a flower with a butterfly on it, two dogs sitting in a basket...The point of the exercise was to teach us how to assess a photograph, decipher the meaning behind each work, and eventually determine whether it was good or bad.
